Dear Parents,
As soon as the child becomes 3 or 4 year old, every parent gets anxious as to which School they should send their child. The foremost concern in the mind of each parent is the safety and security of their child in the School. Every now and then all of us hear about the child abuse not only at School premises but also in the School buses. News about the child drawn in the swimming pool or in the septic tank of the School also frightens the parents. It brings fear not only in the minds of parents but also in the child. This has an ever lasting impact on the over-all development of the child. By remaining in constant fear, the child can loose confidence and suffer disassociation, anxiety, difficulty in concentration and therefore, may not be able to develop properly. Every child has a right for a safe childhood.


It is not possible for the School to keep an eye on each child. Every child is precious for the parents and the country as well. When the child goes to School, the parents remain worried till the child comes home safely.

This can also have a bad effect on the mental health of the parents as well. Due to constant anxiety, the parents may not concentrate on their work.






In order to overcome this problem, we have developed a technology which enables parents to keep an eye on their child not only in the School premises but also while the child is travelling in the School Bus. Besides this, they will receive regular messages as and when the child enters into the School bus and also when he enters into the gate of the School. Similarly, they will get the messages when the child leaves School and gets into the bus. They can watch the live broadcast of the happenings around their child on their phones/computer.

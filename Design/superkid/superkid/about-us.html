Our Vision


Our vision is to ensure peace of mind to parents particularly working parents, who are always worried about the safety of their kids in school, while they are at work. This affects parents health and career both.

Our Mission


Our mission is to provide technology-enabled, simple-to-operate real time system in the hands of parents of small children. As we continue to hear the news of small kids being harassed in school transport or within school premises, it strengthens our resolve to save our kids from such insecurities. With the support of parents, we are sure we can do it.

Our Objective


We intend to cover at least 100 playschools and 100 nursery schools this year. We will guarantee for 3600safety of small kids in these schools. During next session we intend to cover schools in whole of north India.
